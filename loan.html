Loan FAQs

The documents vary based on loan type but generally include:
- Identity proof (PAN, Aadhaar, Passport)
- Address proof (Utility bills, Rental agreement)
- Income proof (Salary slips, Bank statements, IT returns)
- Property documents (for secured loans)
- Photographs
Our team will provide you with a customized checklist based on your specific loan requirement.

The processing time depends on the loan type and completeness of your documentation. Typically:
- Personal loans: 2-5 working days
- Home loans: 7-15 working days
- Business loans: 5-10 working days
We expedite the process by ensuring your application is complete and properly documented from the beginning.

Several factors influence your loan interest rate:
- Credit score/history
- Income level and stability
- Existing debt obligations
- Loan amount and tenure
- Type of loan (secured vs unsecured)
- Market conditions and lender policies
We help you present your financial profile in the best possible way to secure favorable rates.

Most loans allow prepayment, but terms vary:
- Personal loans: Usually allow prepayment after 6-12 months, may have 2-5% charges
- Home loans: Can be prepaid, charges vary by lender (some have zero charges for floating rate loans)
- Business loans: Prepayment terms depend on the agreement
We'll explain all prepayment options and charges before you finalize your loan so there are no surprises later.

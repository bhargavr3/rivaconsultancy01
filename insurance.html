Insurance FAQs

Choosing the right insurance depends on your specific needs, risk profile, and budget. Our experts will assess your situation, explain different policy options, and recommend coverage that provides optimal protection without overpaying for unnecessary features. We consider factors like your age, health, assets, liabilities, and financial goals.

We'll help identify all available discounts to reduce your premiums while maintaining adequate coverage.

Contact us immediately when you need to make a claim. We'll guide you through the entire process, help complete necessary paperwork, and liaise with the insurance company on your behalf. For motor claims, document the scene with photos. For health claims, keep all medical bills and reports. The sooner you inform us, the smoother the claims process will be.

We recommend reviewing your insurance policies annually or whenever you experience major life changes like marriage, having children, buying a home, changing jobs, or significant changes in income. Regular reviews ensure your coverage keeps pace with your evolving needs and takes advantage of new products or better rates in the market.

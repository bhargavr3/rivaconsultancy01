Documents Required for PAN Card

The documents needed depend on whether you're an individual, company, or other entity:

Proof of Identity (Aadhaar, Passport, Voter ID, Driving License)
Proof of Address (Utility bill, Bank statement, Rental agreement)
Date of Birth proof (Birth certificate, School leaving certificate)
Passport size photographs (2 copies)
For companies: Incorporation certificate, MOA/AOA
For HUF: HUF deed and Karta's identity proof

PAN Card FAQs

Typically, it takes 7-15 working days to receive your PAN card after application submission. With our expedited services, we can often get it processed faster. The actual time may vary depending on government processing times and document verification.

PAN (Permanent Account Number) is a unique 10-digit alphanumeric identifier for taxpayers. TAN (Tax Deduction and Collection Account Number) is required for entities that deduct or collect tax at source. While PAN is for all taxpayers, TAN is specifically for tax deductors.

Yes, you can apply for PAN without Aadhaar by submitting other valid identity proofs like passport, voter ID, driving license, etc. However, linking Aadhaar with PAN is mandatory for filing income tax returns, so we recommend providing Aadhaar details if available.

If your PAN card is lost, you can apply for a duplicate PAN card by submitting Form 49A along with identity and address proofs. There is a nominal fee for duplicate PAN card issuance. We can help you with the entire process quickly and efficiently.

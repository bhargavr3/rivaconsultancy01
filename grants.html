Funding Assistance FAQs

Businesses of all sizes and across various sectors may find suitable funding programs. This includes startups, small and medium enterprises, manufacturers, exporters, technology companies, women-owned businesses, and more. We assess each business individually to identify potential opportunities.

The timeline varies depending on the specific program and your business readiness. Simple applications might take a few weeks to prepare, while complex proposals could require several months. We recommend starting the process well before any deadlines to ensure sufficient time for preparation and review.

Requirements vary by program but commonly include business registration documents, financial statements, tax returns, project proposals, business plans, and ownership details. We provide a complete checklist based on the specific programs you're applying to and help gather all necessary documentation.

Our fee structure varies based on the complexity of the application and the services required. We typically offer both project-based pricing and hourly consulting options. During our initial consultation, we'll provide transparent pricing information based on your specific needs. Some fees may be contingent upon successful funding approval.
